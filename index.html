Welcome to Mount Egmont School........


Mount Egmont School is situated in an extremely healthy environment. The location provides a beautiful scenery ideal for education. The school is open to boys and girls of all communities who would like to benefit from the particular type of education and training provided. The school aims at a sound basis of education along with modern lines and the programmes of instruction is designed to produce a sense of vocation and spirit of service to the county. Care is taken to develop integrity of character, initiative and courtesy. Moral instruction is imparted to all pupils. The object being to make them worthy citizen of India.



Location:-

Mount Egmont School is located at foot hills of Canary.The School is situated about 5kms from Hazaribagh bus stand, 60 kms from Koderma railway station.


Scouts:-

Scouting is a year round activity where the Troops meet regularly and has various achievements to its credit.The scouts have made the school proud by winning several awards.


Conveyance:-

School has seven busses which goes in seven diffrent routes.The charges are applied as per rates prevalent in the current year.Pickup point and route can be enquired from school's admin office.


Boarding:-

Mount Egmont is one of the oldest boarding school in Hazaribagh.It has total of five dormitories. Special dormitory for small boys.


Physical Development:-

The school's sporting activities consist of cricket, boxing, athletics, volleyball, table tennis, badminton and indoor games.Yoga is practised at school and we have dedicated yoga guru at school. We take our sports very seriously and are considered to be an integral part of the school curriculum and every oppertunity is given to the pupil to take an active part in games at school or interschool level. Games are played on an Inter-Class, Inter-House and Inter-School Level.


Security:-

Close circuit cameras are installed in the campus which are monitored from two location.Fire extinguishers are installed at each floor of the building.Disaster management practices are adhered religiously.
